About Us

Welcome to our tournament organizing platform! We are a passionate team from Nagaland, dedicated to bringing exciting mobile gaming tournaments to life. Our primary focus is on popular games like Mobile Legends, Clash of Clans, and BGMI. We aim to create a competitive and fun environment for gamers to showcase their skills and connect with the community.

Join us in our mission to elevate the mobile gaming experience and make every tournament a memorable event. Stay tuned for upcoming tournaments and get ready to battle it out!
